Game Tips

Orisa

Peek, bait, retreat

Fortify -> spin -> fire -> throw -> reset

Role identity: You’re the anchor. You own the frontline and deny enemy space by forcing bad angles and absorbing key cooldowns. Trade Fortify for one enemy cooldown Use corners - peek, bait, retreat Push in waves — 2–3 seconds of pressure, then reset behind cover. Your gun isn’t poke — it’s pressure. Don’t spray all game. Always anchor between your supports and DPS, not far ahead. Core rhythm: Fortify → Spin (as Fortify ends) → pressure fire → Javelin Throw → WAIT → repeat
Junker Queen

Push, pause, punish, retreat

Shout -> swing wide of corner -> knife -> axe -> shotgun -> reset

Role identity: You’re pressure + sustain Landing your Jagged Blade pull defines your tempo Wait for cooldowns → dive → hit all → back out Shout → swing around cover → secure knife → then push in Core rhythm: Shout → Swing out from cover → Knife / Knife Pull → Axe → Shotgun finish. Reset behind cover
Moira

Fade for escape only

Stay within 2 seconds of cover

Focus the same target as the one who you're healing is targeting Look past the tank and do damage on support, then dps, then tank Your value comes from sustain + consistent DPS, not flashy kills
Zenyatta

Keep heal orb on your tank, and discord orb on enemy tank (otherwise discord who you're shooting)

Cover > charge right click > peek & volley damage > hide back behind cover

Your job is to apply pressure, try and get picks
All heros

“I’m not trying to kill — I’m trying to control.”

“Peek → bait something → hide → peek during their downtime.” You fight in bursts, not continuous exposure.

Don’t dive or push alone — push with purpose and support line-of-sight If your supports can’t see you, you’re not creating space — you’re feeding. Think of yourself as the engine of the team: start, stop, and pace the fight.
