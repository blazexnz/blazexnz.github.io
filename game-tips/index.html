Game Tips

Orisa

Peek, bait, retreat

Fortify → Spin → Fire → Throw → Reset

Role identity: You’re the anchor. You own the frontline and deny enemy space by forcing bad angles and absorbing key cooldowns.

Trade Fortify for one enemy cooldown.

Use corners – peek, bait, retreat.

Push in waves — 2–3 seconds of pressure, then reset behind cover.

Your gun isn’t poke — it’s pressure. Don’t spray all game.

Always anchor between your supports and DPS, not far ahead.

Core rhythm: Fortify → Spin (as Fortify ends) → Pressure Fire → Javelin Throw → WAIT → Repeat.

Junker Queen

Push, pause, punish, retreat

Shout → Swing wide of corner → Knife → Axe → Shotgun → Reset

Role identity: You’re pressure + sustain.

Main tempo:Push → Pause → Punish → Retreat.

Other notes: Landing your Jagged Blade pull defines your tempo.

Core rhythm: Shout → Swing out from cover → Knife / Knife Pull → Axe → Shotgun finish. Reset behind cover.

Winston

Dive in, force cooldowns, disengage safely, and re-engage with purpose.

Jump in → Bubble → Zap → Jump out → Reset → Repeat

Role identity: You're the fight starter. Dive, create chaos, break setups, open space for DPS to follow through.

Always ensure:

  • You’re in healer LOS
  • You have bubble ready
  • You know your exit
  • Your team can follow up

Core rhythm: Jump in → Bubble → Zap → Jump out → Reset → Repeat.

General tank

“I’m not trying to kill — I’m trying to control.”

“Peek → bait something → hide → peek during their downtime.” You fight in bursts, not continuous exposure.

Don’t dive or push alone — push with purpose and support line-of-sight.

If your supports can’t see you, you’re not creating space — you’re feeding.

Think of yourself as the engine of the team: start, stop, and pace the fight.

Moira

Fade for escape only

Use cover (mid-range) heal → poke → retreat → repeat.

Off angles

Role Identity: Sustain + damage support.

Main Tempo: Mid-range → heal → poke → retreat → repeat.

If in doubt: Stay at mid-range and heal priority targets; don’t overcommit.

Other Key Ideas: Fade = survival/escape, not panic. Damage beam = charge + pressure. Coalescence = fight-decider.

Most Important:

  • Heal efficiently, prioritise survival.
  • Use Fade wisely.

Focus the same target as the one your heal target is focusing.

Look past the tank and do damage on support, then DPS, then tank.

Your value comes from sustain + consistent DPS, not flashy kills.

Zenyatta

Keep heal orb on your tank, and discord orb on enemy tank (otherwise discord who you're shooting).

Cover → Charge right click → Peek & volley damage → Hide back behind cover

Role Identity: High-value poke healer. Deny healing, apply Discord, sustain with Harmony.

Main Tempo: Poke → Discord → Reposition → Repeat.

Other Key Ideas: Discord = fight-decider. Harmony = pre-emptive sustain. Stay safe; Zen dies quickly.

If in doubt: Stay at range behind cover and apply Harmony/Discord safely.

  • Keep Discord on high-value targets.
  • Stay safe while healing.
Kiriko

TP to safety early

Hold Suzu for the BIGGEST THREAT, not random poke

Role Identity: Mobility + poke healer. Keep allies alive and pressure flankers.

Main Tempo: Heal → Teleport → Kunai poke → Protection Suzu → Repeat.

Other Key Ideas: Teleport = reposition/save. Suzu = reactive save. Track ally positions.

If in doubt: Heal and stay mobile; save Suzu for high-value ally.

  • Hold Suzu for the BIGGEST THREAT, not random poke.
  • TP to safety early.
General support

Survival

Don't be greedy for kills

Use off angles.

Warm up

Be deliberate

  1. Tracking the target without shooting
  2. Deliberate shots – aim for 100% accuracy, use a metronome
  3. 180 flicks to target acquisition
  4. Always intend for head shots
  5. Focus on the target not just look at it (eg. blade on fan, not fan itself)
  6. Hero specific cooldown aim training (eg. Ana sleep dart, Orisa jav, Junker Queen jagged blade)
  7. 50–100 kills
