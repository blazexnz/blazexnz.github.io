# Structures of the Rosary

This page gives you a the structure for each mode.

- [Standard](#standard)
- [For deceased](#for-deceased)
- [One decade)[#one-decade]

## Standard
### Opening prayers
- signOfTheCross
- apostlesCreed
- ourFather
- threeHailMarys
- gloryBe

### Meditation prayers
- mystery
- ourFather
- tenHailMarys
- gloryBe
- fatimaPrayer

### Closing Prayers
- hailHolyQueen
- closingDialogue
- closingPrayer
- signOfTheCross

## For deceased
### Opening prayers
- signOfTheCross
- apostlesCreed
- forDeceasedIntention
- prayerForTheFaithfulDeparted
- ourFather
- threeHailMarys
- gloryBe

### Meditation prayers
- mystery
- ourFather
- tenHailMarys
- gloryBe
- fatimaPrayer
- prayerForTheFaithfulDeparted

### Closing Prayers
- hailHolyQueen
- closingDialogue
- closingPrayer
- litanyOfTheBlessedVirginMary
- concludingPrayerForTheDeceased
- signOfTheCross

## One decade
### Opening prayers
- signOfTheCross
- apostlesCreed
- yourPersonalIntention

### Meditation prayers
- ourFather
- tenHailMarys
- gloryBe
- fatimaPrayer

### Closing Prayers
- signOfTheCross
